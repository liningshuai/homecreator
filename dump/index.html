About Us

Home Creator online is established to provide you with the highest value for money in plantation shutters, roller blinds and all other window furnishings. We have over 50 years of combined experience in the design of quality materials and componentry to provide you with cutting edge technology for user simplicity and aesthetically complimenting any décor in your home.

We choose to have plantation shutters, PVC shutters, roller blinds and bamboo & jute roman blinds manufactured overseas as this keeps the cost down in a market that has become very price conscious. Curtains are made in Australia. Our commitment to continue providing quality in the manufacturing of our products is not compromised.

High quality, lower price and good service is our company’s mission.

Thank you for your visiting and orders.

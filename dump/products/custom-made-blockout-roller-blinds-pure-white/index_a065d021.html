Care and Maintenance

Operating Roller Blinds

  • To raise the blinds, please stand directly in front of the chain, making sure the chain does not rub against the chain wheel mechanism
  • Avoid exposure to wind or rain, it is hard to remove stain from fabrics

Â

Cleaning Roller Blinds

  • Regular clean dust with a soft feather duster or a vacuum cleaner
  • Spot or stain clean with a soft brush, damp cloth or sponge with warm soapy water gently remove the spot or stain and leaving the blinds down and dry
  • Make sure cloth or sponge is not too wet to leave water marks, and not to crease the fabrics
  • Use art gum eraser to remove some spots or marks
